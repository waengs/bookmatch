Why You Should Read

In 2019, a study published in the journal Neurology found that reading books, contributed to a decreased rate of cognitive decline by approximately 32%.

A study published in the journal Social Science & Medicine found that book readers have a 20% lower risk of mortality over a 12-year period compared to non-readers.

According to a study at the University of Sussex, reading for just six minutes can reduce stress levels by up to 68%.
